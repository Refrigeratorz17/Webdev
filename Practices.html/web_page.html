What is Honkai Star Rail? ( ╹ -╹)?

Honkai: Star Rail is a free to play role-playing game with aspects of space and fantasy. What makes it special is its turn-based combat, an adventure driven story, exploration of several worlds, battling enemies, solving puzzles, and uncovering mysteries of the universe.

Honkai: Star Rail Wiki

Gameplay

Honkai: Star Rail's gameplay combines exploration of many worlds with strategic turn-based combat. During combat you have options to select certain actions, such as basic attacks, skills, and ultimates. You manage team composition, skill points, and character roles. The player builds a team of four characters, each with unique abilities and stories. Each character belongs to a specific combat type and path, which define their roles in battle.

Story

The player takes on the role of the Trailblazer, a character experiencing a total loss of memory, who travels the galaxy on the Astral Express(a spaceship). The story involves uncovering secrets of worlds, learning and meeting characters, solving conflicts of stories, and experiencing the dark and light moments.

Gacha Sytem

If you don't know already, a gacha game uses a 'gacha' or a 'loot box' mechanic to reward the player with randomized virtual items such as charcters and equipment. You use in game currency that can be earned through gameplay or purchased with real money to preform 'pulls' that give a random reward. The gacha system whithin Honkai: Star Rail is called the Warp, where players use Stellar Jades(the ingame currency) to pull for characters or Light Cones(weapons). It features a pity system that guarentees a 5-star item after a certain amount of pulls. It also features different banners with limited-time characters and some permanent standard banners.

Honkai series

The Hoyoverse(global entertainment brand and publishing subsidiary of the Chinese company miHoYo) Honkai series is a shared multiverse of games developed by miHoY, these games, along with other related media, are connected through a shared cosmology(The scientific study of the origin, development, structure and future of the entire universe) but take place in seperate worlds. The main games in the "Honkai" series are

  • Honkai Impact 3rd
  • Honkai: Star Rail
  • Honkai: Nexus Anima(in development)
