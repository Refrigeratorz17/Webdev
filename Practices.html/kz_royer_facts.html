Visual arts.

I am Kayzee Royer and one thing I really like is art! Specifcally visual arts. The definiton for visual arts is, creative art whose products are to be appreciated by sight, such as painting, scuplture and film.(as contrasted with literature and music). Visual arts usually expresses/holds a meaning, emotion and ideas. The main goal of visual arts is to create a visual experience that can be aesthetically pleasing, thought-provoking, or functional. Growing up.

I grew up always surrounded by the arts. My mom enjoyed sketching, using reference photos to recreate realistic potraits. My Aunt loved to paint, to crochet, and to draw, teaching me how to let my creativity loose on a paper or canvas. My other aunt would print me out coloring pages to teach me about colors, and so much more. I've always liked the way colors and different shapes could express emotions and feelings I couldn't put into words. It also takes effort and creativity, showing how hard someone worked to make everything harmonize together. I started drawing in sketchbooks and truly trying to dedicate my time to art when i was around th ages of five to seven, watching videos and looking at references to enhance my skill. It's always fun to look back through my old sketchbooks and realize how far i've come.

How art has helped me.

We all go through hard times and face the troubles of life, when I went through This art is what helped me to figure everything out. My parents divorced when I was little and since then it had seemed everything was only getting worse. From family issues, mental problems, and rough experiences, it had truly left an imprint in my life. When things got tough art was always there to, if not help me, guide me. I would draw charcters and my interests to make me happy, I would paint landscapes and experiment with different mediums to feed my curiosity. I have grown quite a bit, and I have a lot more growing to do, but as of right now i'm happy with the way my life is, and I have art to thank for that. It taught me empathy and creativity, it helped me make friends and find my emotions. This is the reason why art is so important to me.
