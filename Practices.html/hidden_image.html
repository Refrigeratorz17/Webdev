Why cats?

A cat is a domesticated, small, carnivorous mammil kept as a pet, known for its agility, soft fur and hunting instincts. At the moment I have three cats, Shadow is our Calico cat that has dark fur with some brown spots. And Lila is our shorthaired cat with grey fur and bright green eyes. I have had a couple other cats, Lilith, Milo, and Hobo. I like cats because they can chill, crazy, and fun all at the same time. And from my experience they take way less care than dogs.

It's an on going debate on whether cats or dogs are better, and my answer is both. Despite this website being about cats, I also love dogs. When I had my cat Milo, my dog Indie, who is a chihuahua loved him. They got along really well and loved to play and spend time with eachother. All the cats that I have had love cuddling and even if they might not be pretty social, they aren't mean. I love cats so much!
